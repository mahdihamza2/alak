GGT Petrochemical is dedicated to supplying high-quality raw materials for factories worldwide while supporting businesses in exporting their products efficiently. Since entering the international trade arena in Turkey in 2006, our operations initially centered on exporting petrochemicals and select domestic goods to neighboring markets such as Iraq, the UAE, Qatar, and Ukraine. Over the years, our reach has expanded, and we now collaborate with a trusted network of specialized petrochemical suppliers across the Middle East.

With a team of skilled experts and experienced consultants, GGT is committed to delivering exceptional service marked by speed, cost-efficiency, and uncompromising quality. Our deep industry knowledge and customer-focused approach have empowered us to meet client demands swiftly and reliably, serving factories across Europe and the United States with confidence and consistency.
